About StudySnap

StudySnap is a full‑stack MERN application that combines social networking with academic productivity. Students can connect with classmates, earn StudyTokens through daily activities, access a rich library of study materials, and watch professor‑approved video lectures. Professors have a dedicated dashboard to upload videos, and administrators manage users, colleges, courses, and moderate content. The platform includes real‑time chat, group discussions, and a unique ID card scanner that rewards consistency.

✨ Key Features

📱 ID Card Scanner

Scan your college ID (QR/1D barcode) to earn StudyTokens. First scan 5 tokens, subsequent scans based on time gap – max 2 scans/day. Fallback manual entry available.

🎮 Gamification

Earn tokens by checking in daily, scanning ID, taking quizzes, uploading unique PDFs, and completing missions. Track your streak and token history.

📚 E‑Library

Upload and browse PDF study materials, filtered by college and course. Get 5 tokens for each new upload. Search and view materials with ease.

🎥 Video Lectures

Professors upload video files (up to 100MB). Admin approves before they appear to students. Watch lectures on professor profiles or in the video library.

👥 Connect & Chat

Search classmates, send connection requests, accept/reject. Real‑time chat with connected users – share PDFs and text. Also create study groups with group chat.

📊 Admin Dashboard

Full control over students, professors, colleges, courses, and video approvals. Send notifications to students. View platform statistics.
